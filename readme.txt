== Description ==

Do you know including User Generated Content (UGC) can effectively increase the engagement of your visitors?

Our hashtag photo wall is a very simple and straight forward to use wordpress plug-in which helps you to crate UGC in a few clicks. You can create a photo wall on your website by instagram hashtag and play around with your photos.

Have fun creating UGC! And please let us know if you have any questions: consultant@alluatech.com
